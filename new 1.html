Hajj and Umrah Travel Packages

Every year, millions of Muslims from around the world travel to Mecca, Saudi Arabia to perform the pilgrimages of Hajj and Umrah. Traveling to Mecca, especially during Hajj season, may involve a lot of hassle, and it can also be very costly. As such, many Muslims prefer to purchase Hajj and Umrah travel packages to make their pilgrimages more efficient, comfortable, and affordable. If you are a Pakistani who is planning to perform Hajj and Umrah soon, it is advisable that you look for a travel agent that offers Hajj and Umrah travel services.

Basic Information about Hajj and Umrah

Hajj refers to an Islamic pilgrimage to the holy city of Mecca, and it is considered one of the five pillars of Islam. This pilgrimage takes place on the 8th, 12th, or 13th of the last month in the Islamic calendar every year. It is a compulsory religious duty for all adult Muslims that must be performed at least once in their lifetimes, unless they are physically or financially incapable. The Hajj is the world’s largest yearly gathering of people.

Unlike Hajj, Umrah is not a mandatory pilgrimage, but it is highly recommended. It is often referred to as the “minor pilgrimage”, with Hajj being the “major pilgrimage”. Umrah can be performed any time of the year, and it can be carried out in conjunction with Hajj, or independently of Hajj.

Why You Need to Hire a Hajj and Umrah Travel Agent?

While Pakistan is located not very far from Mecca, there are a lot of problems that you will encounter as you undertake your spiritual journey. First of all, you need to apply for visa, which can be a big hassle if you are traveling during Hajj season. Booking flights and accommodation can also be difficult during that time of the year, because there are millions of other Muslims like you traveling to Mecca. Even if you manage to get the air tickets and accommodation you need, you will most likely have to pay very high prices. There is also the problem of getting around the city once you are there. Being unfamiliar with the culture and rules of the country can get you into trouble.

If you purchase Hajj and Umrah packages from a travel agent, all you have to do is pack your bag and follow the schedule. Your travel agent will help you apply for visa, book your flights and accommodation, and provide transportation and meals in Mecca. Additionally, he will also teach you how to perform Hajj and Umrah rituals, and let you know what you can and cannot do in Saudi Arabia. If you encounter any problem while you are in Mecca, your travel agent will be there to help you. Also, it will cost significantly less to purchase Hajj and Umrah travel packages than traveling on your own.

How to Choose the Right Hajj and Umrah Travel Agent?

There are numerous travel agents in Pakistan offering Hajj and Umrah packages, but not all of them are trustworthy. When you are choosing a Hajj and Umrah travel agent, it is important that you look for the following things.

  • Registered travel agent
  • Professional behavior
  • Transparency between agent and customers
  • Good reviews from previous customers
  • Partnership with established hotels and travel companies
  • Complete assistance throughout the trip
  • Transfers and tour guide
  • Budget-friendly packages
  • Tailor-made packages
